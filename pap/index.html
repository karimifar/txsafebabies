Parenting Action Plan (PAP) Training and Implementation

The videos and downloads on this page are designed to (1) help clinics implement the Parenting Action Plan (PAP) and (2) train clinic staff to administer the Parenting Action Plan to new mothers.

If you are an administrator making implementation decisions about the Parenting Action Plan, we recommend that you watch Module 1, Module 2, and download all worksheets.

If you are the clinic staff who will be administering the Parenting Action Plan to new mothers, you must watch Module 1, Module 3, Module 4 and the PAP in Action videos.

After you have watched the training videos, you must acknowledge that you have completed training and have watched the appropriate videos. UTHSCT will send printed Parenting Action Plans to the clinic after (1) at least one clinic staff has acknowledge (http://j.mp/2vtc6xd) that training has been completed and (2) the clinic has uploaded (https://is.gd/papworksheet) the clinic flow and the plan of action worksheet.
